Step 1: Choose Your Build

Pick a strong, balanced build like: Portal + Godhuman + Gravity Cane or Shark Anchor + Skull Guitar + Angel/Ghoul V4. This helps you stun, combo, and chase easily.

Step 2: Learn Combos

Start with small combos (techs) if you're a beginner. Avoid one-shot combos until you're experienced. Ice, Portal, Dough, and Kitsune are great fruits for this.

Step 3: Master Kentrick

Turn instinct OFF until a move comes that can be dodged. Learn when to use it. For example, avoid Shark Anchor X by turning on Instinct just before it lands. PvP more and you'll naturally learn the moves that cancel or avoid Instinct.

Step 4: Go Bounty Hunting

Now test your skills. Challenge strong bounty players. If you want me to personally train you, request coaching below!
